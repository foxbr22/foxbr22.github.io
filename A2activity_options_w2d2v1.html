Technology Phrasal Verbs

Explanations

Log in - To access a computer system or online account by entering a username and password.

Example: "Please log in to your account to view the meeting details."

Log out - To exit a computer system or online account.

Example: "Don't forget to log out of your account when you're finished."

Sign up - To register or create an account on a website or service.

Example: "You need to sign up for the webinar by filling out the form."

Turn up - To increase the volume.

Example: "Can you turn up the volume? I can't hear you."

Break up - When the audio or video quality becomes poor and intermittent during a call.

Example: "Your voice is breaking up; can you repeat that?"
