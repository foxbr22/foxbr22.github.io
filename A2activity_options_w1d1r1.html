Reading Comprehension Exercise

Last weekend, I took my daughter to the park. She brought her favorite bag with her. It was a sunny day, and we enjoyed walking and talking. She told me about her school and friends, and we had a lovely time together.

While we were sitting on a bench, a boy came by with his own bag. He showed us his toys inside and said proudly, "These toys are mine." My daughter smiled and showed him her bag, saying, "This bag is mine, and it has my favorite things in it." They started playing and sharing their toys, laughing and having fun.

When it was time to leave, my daughter asked, "Whose bag is that on the bench?" I looked and saw it was hers. I said, "That bag is yours, my dear. Don't forget to take it with you." She grabbed her bag and hugged it tightly, saying, "This bag is definitely mine." She was so happy to have it with her as we walked back home, talking about the fun day we had.

1. Where did the narrator take his daughter last weekend?

A) To the zoo
B) To the park
C) To the beach
D) To the mall

2. What did the narrator's daughter bring with her to the park?

A) Her favorite book
B) Her favorite toy
C) Her favorite bag
D) Her favorite hat

3. What was the weather like on the day they went to the park?

A) Rainy
B) Cloudy
C) Windy
D) Sunny

4. What did the narrator's daughter talk about while walking with her father?

A) Her school and friends
B) Her favorite TV show
C) Her pet
D) Her new clothes

5. Who approached them while they were sitting on a bench?

A) A girl with a doll
B) A boy with his own bag
C) A man with a dog
D) A woman with a baby

6. What did the boy show them?

A) His books
B) His snacks
C) His toys inside his bag
D) His drawings

7. What did the narrator's daughter show the boy?

A) Her new shoes
B) Her drawing
C) Her bag with her favorite things
D) Her pet

8. Whose toys were inside the boy's bag?

A) The daughter's
B) The boy's
C) The narrator's
D) The girl's

9. Whose bag was on the bench when it was time to leave?

A) The narrator's
B) The boy's
C) The daughter's
D) Another child's

10. How did the narrator's daughter react when she found out the bag was hers?

A) She left it on the bench.
B) She gave it to the boy.
C) She hugged it tightly and said, "This bag is definitely mine."
D) She cried because she lost it.
