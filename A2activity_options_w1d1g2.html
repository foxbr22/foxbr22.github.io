The verb 'be' - Activities

1. Basic Meaning

1. What does the verb 'be' express?

a) Action
b) Existence or state of being
c) Possession

2. Which sentence uses 'be' to express a state?

a) She runs fast.
b) They have a car.
c) The sky is blue.

3. In the sentence "There are five apples", what does 'are' express?

a) Action
b) Existence
c) Possession

4. What does 'be' express in "I am a student"?

a) A temporary action
b) A state of being
c) A past event

2. Uses

5. Which sentence uses 'be' to describe someone?

a) She works as a teacher.
b) They have three cats.
c) He is tall and handsome.

6. How is 'be' used in "The book is on the table"?

a) To describe the book
b) To state where the book is
c) To tell the time

7. Which sentence uses 'be' to tell the time?

a) The clock is broken.
b) We have five minutes left.
c) It is 3 o'clock.

8. Which sentence uses 'be' in a passive structure?

a) They are building a house.
b) The house was built in 1990.
c) We are at home.

3. Questions

9. How do we form questions with 'be'?

a) By adding 'do' before the subject
b) By putting 'be' before the noun/pronoun
c) By adding 'not' after 'be'

10. Which is a correct question form?

a) Is she happy?
b) Does she is happy?
c) She is happy?

11. What's the correct question form of "You are a student."?

a) You are a student?
b) Are you a student?
c) Do you are a student?

12. What's the correct question form for "It was cold yesterday."?

a) Was it cold yesterday?
b) Did it was cold yesterday?
c) It was cold yesterday?

4. Negative Form

13. How do we form negatives with 'be'?

a) By adding 'don't' before 'be'
b) By adding 'not' after 'be'
c) By adding 'no' before the sentence

14. Which is the correct negative form of "I am tired."?

a) I am not tired.
b) I don't am tired.
c) I no am tired.

15. What's the negative form of "She is here."?

a) She isn't here.
b) She doesn't is here.
c) She not is here.

16. What's the correct negative form of "It was raining."?

a) It wasn't raining.
b) It didn't was raining.
c) It not was raining.

5. Contractions

17. What is the contraction of "she is"?

a) she's
b) shes
c) she'is

18. Which is NOT a correct contraction?

a) I'm
b) you're
c) they'ren't

19. What's the correct contraction for "is not"?

a) is'nt
b) isn't
c) isnt

20. What's the correct contraction in the question "I'm doing well in this class, ___ I?"

a) am'nt
b) ain't
c) aren't
