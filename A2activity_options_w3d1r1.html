Reading Comprehension: The Big Game

The Final Match

The big game of the season was here, and everyone was very excited. The team's coach had spent many weeks helping the players get ready. They practiced every move and plan. The court was full of fans cheering loudly for their teams. As the game began, the score was close, with both teams playing hard to win.

In the first half, the home team had trouble playing well. The crowd was worried, and you could hear quiet talks when the other team scored. Seeing this, the coach called a timeout. "Guess what?" he said, looking at each player. "I believe in you all. We have practiced for this moment. Let's win this game!"

Feeling motivated, the team played with more energy. The fans, seeing the change, cheered even louder. Each time the home team scored, the fans clapped and shouted happily. The players worked well together, showing how good teamwork can make a difference.

When the game ended, the home team had won. The fans were very happy and celebrated the great win and the end of the season.

Comprehension Questions

1. What is the passage about?

2. Who helped the players get ready for the game?

3. How was the atmosphere as the game started?

4. What did the coach do when the home team was not playing well?

5. What did the coach say to motivate the team during the timeout?

6. How did the fans react after the team started playing better?

7. What does the story suggest about teamwork?

8. How did the game end and how did the fans react?
