Possessive Adjectives - Activities

1. Which possessive adjective is used with "I"?

a) your
b) my
c) his

2. Complete the sentence: "She loves ___ job."

a) his
b) her
c) its

3. Which sentence uses the possessive adjective correctly?

a) The cat licked paw its.
b) The cat licked its paw.
c) The cat licked it's paw.

4. What's the possessive adjective for "they"?

a) its
b) theirs
c) their

5. Choose the correct sentence:

a) This is mine book.
b) This is my book.
c) This is me book.

6. What's the possessive adjective in "The dog wagged ___ tail"?

a) it's
b) its
c) it

7. Which is NOT a possessive adjective?

a) our
b) your
c) ours

8. Complete the sentence: "We forgot ___ umbrellas at home."

a) ours
b) our
c) we
