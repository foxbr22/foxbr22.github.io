Emma: Hey, John! Long time no see!

John: Emma! It's been ages. How have you been?

Emma: I've been great, thanks. Busy with work and all. How about you?

John: Same here. Work has been keeping me on my toes. By the way, do you know Sarah? She's the host of the party.

Emma: Yes, I do! We worked together on a project last year. Have you met her yet?

John: Not yet. I was just about to go find her and say hello. Want to come with me?

Emma: Sure, let's go. It's always nice to catch up with her.

John: Absolutely. So, any exciting plans for the weekend?

Emma: Just relaxing and maybe catching up on some reading. You?

John: Sounds nice. I'm thinking of going hiking if the weather is good.

Emma: That sounds fun! I should try that sometime. Anyway, let's go find Sarah.
