Possessive Pronouns and 'Whose' - Exercises

1. Complete the sentence: "This book is ___."

a) my
b) mine
c) me

2. Choose the correct possessive pronoun: "The red car is ___."

a) her
b) hers
c) she

3. Fill in the blank: "___ is this pen?"

a) Who
b) Whom
c) Whose

4. Select the correct sentence:

a) That laptop is your.
b) That laptop is yours.
c) That laptop is you.

5. Complete the sentence: "The dog wagged ___ tail."

a) it's
b) its
c) it

6. Choose the correct sentence:

a) The blue hat is my.
b) The blue hat is mine.
c) The blue hat is me.

7. Fill in the blank: "I don't know ___ book this is."

a) who's
b) whose
c) whom

8. Select the correct possessive pronoun: "Is this car ___?"

a) they
b) their
c) theirs
Great job! You've mastered possessive pronouns and 'whose'!
